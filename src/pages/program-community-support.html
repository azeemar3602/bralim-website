🫶 Community Support

Introduction

About this programme

When you arrive in a new country, the difference between struggling and settling is often a community that has your back. Our Community Support programme welcomes new arrivals and stands with members through the milestones and challenges of life in the Netherlands.

Photo: Community Support
Objectives

What we aim to achieve

  • Help new arrivals settle and feel at home
  • Offer guidance on practical everyday matters
  • Provide a support network in times of need
  • Foster friendships that reduce isolation
Activities

What we do

  • Welcome sessions and buddy pairing for newcomers
  • Practical information on life in the Netherlands
  • Peer support and check-ins for members
  • Community response during difficult times
🙋

How to Participate

Reach out to us to join, request support, or volunteer as a community buddy. Every member is welcome — whether you need support today or want to offer it to others.
