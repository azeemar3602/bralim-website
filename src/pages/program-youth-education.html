🎓 Youth & Education

Introduction

About this programme

Our young people are the future of the community. This programme provides mentoring, tutoring and opportunities that help youth flourish academically, socially and personally.

Photo: Youth & Education
Objectives

What we aim to achieve

  • Support academic success for students
  • Provide mentorship and positive role models
  • Build confidence, identity and leadership
  • Open doors to opportunities and networks
Activities

What we do

  • Mentorship pairing with community professionals
  • Homework help and tutoring support
  • Youth workshops and leadership sessions
  • Careers guidance and skills days
🙋

How to Participate

Enrol a young person, volunteer as a mentor or tutor, or partner with us on youth initiatives. Together we can help every young person reach their potential.
