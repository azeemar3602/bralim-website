This Cookie Policy explains how BRALIM uses cookies and similar technologies on bralim.org.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work and can provide information to the site owners.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies

Required for the website to function (for example, to remember your cookie choice). These are always on.

Analytics cookies (optional)

With your consent, we use analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. These are only set if you accept.

Managing your cookies

When you first visit, we ask for your consent to non-essential cookies. You can accept or decline. You can also control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Third-party cookies

Some features (such as embedded maps or social links) may set their own cookies. Please refer to those providers' policies for details.
