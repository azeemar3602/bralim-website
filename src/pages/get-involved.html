Get Involved

There are many ways to be part of BRALIM. Whether you have time, skills or resources to share, your contribution helps our community thrive.

Why It Matters

Your contribution changes lives

Communities are built by people who show up for one another. When you get involved with BRALIM, you help newcomers settle, young people grow, families connect and traditions live on.

  • Help a newcomer feel welcome and supported
  • Mentor a young person toward their potential
  • Keep Ugandan culture alive for the next generation
  • Strengthen partnerships that expand what we can do
