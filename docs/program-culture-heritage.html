🥁 Culture & Heritage

Introduction

About this programme

Our culture is our compass. This programme keeps Ugandan heritage, language, music, food and traditions alive for our members and shares them proudly with the wider Dutch society.

Photo: Culture & Heritage
Objectives

What we aim to achieve

  • Celebrate Ugandan culture and traditions
  • Pass heritage and language to the next generation
  • Share our culture with the wider community
  • Create moments of joy and belonging
Activities

What we do

  • Cultural celebrations and national day events
  • Music, dance and traditional performances
  • Food festivals and community meals
  • Language and heritage sessions for children
🙋

How to Participate

Join our cultural events, perform, cook, or help us organise. If you carry a tradition worth sharing, we want to celebrate it with you.
