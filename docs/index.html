Together, We Build a Stronger Ugandan Community

Every journey begins with belonging. BRALIM brings together Ugandans living in the Netherlands to celebrate our heritage, strengthen our community and create opportunities for individuals and families to thrive. Whether you are new to the Netherlands or have lived here for many years, BRALIM is your home away from home.

Welcome to BRALIM

Strong communities are built together

BRALIM believes strong communities are built through friendship, volunteerism, shared culture and mutual support.

We organise cultural celebrations, educational programmes, networking events and community initiatives that strengthen relationships and improve lives. From your first Sunday gathering to lifelong friendships, there is a place for you here.

  • Cultural celebrations that keep our heritage alive
  • Educational programmes for youth and families
  • Networking for professionals and entrepreneurs
  • Charitable initiatives at home and here in the Netherlands
