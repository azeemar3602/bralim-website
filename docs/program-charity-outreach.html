🌍 Charity & Outreach

Introduction

About this programme

Giving back is part of who we are. This programme channels the generosity of our community into charitable action that supports people here in the Netherlands and back home in Uganda.

Photo: Charity & Outreach
Objectives

What we aim to achieve

  • Support communities in need here and in Uganda
  • Coordinate fundraising and charitable drives
  • Respond to appeals with compassion
  • Manage resources transparently and ethically
Activities

What we do

  • Fundraising events and charity drives
  • Community outreach and support projects
  • Partnerships with charitable organisations
  • Donation campaigns for causes that matter
🙋

How to Participate

Donate, fundraise, volunteer, or suggest a cause. Every contribution — large or small — makes a real difference.
