Get Involved

There are many ways to be part of BRALIM. Whether you have time, skills or resources to share, your contribution helps our community thrive.

🌟

Become a Member

Join our community to access events, networking, volunteering and newsletters — and to belong to a community that feels like home. Membership is open to everyone who shares our values.

🙌

Volunteer

Give your time and talents to make a difference. Volunteers help run programmes, organise events, mentor youth, welcome newcomers and support charitable initiatives. Every hour counts.

Partner With Us

Are you an organisation, business or institution that shares our vision? Partner with BRALIM to support the community through sponsorship, collaboration or shared initiatives.

Why It Matters

Your contribution changes lives

Communities are built by people who show up for one another. When you get involved with BRALIM, you help newcomers settle, young people grow, families connect and traditions live on.

  • Help a newcomer feel welcome and supported
  • Mentor a young person toward their potential
  • Keep Ugandan culture alive for the next generation
  • Strengthen partnerships that expand what we can do
